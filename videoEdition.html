Video Editing Experience

My Experience with Video creation and editing

Storyboard

For this storyboard I was assigned to create the scene of the character doing homework. Often times when I do homework I get very bored and spend more time with my face on the keyboard than doing homework. To recreate this I added the character taking notes from a lecture, sighing then faceplanting on the laptop out of boredom.

Video Editing

In the video editing assignment I was tasked with recreating my scene from the storyboard. Since faceplanting on my desk is somehting I do very often it was easy to recreate the scene. Overall I am very proud of my work with the video.

VFX

For this assignment I was tasked with creating a unique video effect. When looking around I found a video where you can create a futuristic gun effect so I followed the tutorial and created a unique kill effect for the game Among Us. Overall I am proud of how it came out.
